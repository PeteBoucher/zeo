Your private multiplayer VR server. Connect from any browser, share the URL. Identity carries over across servers (CRD blockchain). Hotload and build with NPM mods — items, games, superpowers, and more — without leaving VR. Works with your headset or mouse + keyboard. Make your own mods and earn CRD with the payments API.

How it makes you more awesome

Own your world

You own your server. Friends can join with a browser URL. You're there together with voice chat. You can use your headset if you're cool, or just mouse + keyboard if you're too cool for a headset. Learn more

Download superpowers

Mod your VR world with drag-and-drop tools, games, and abilities for your avatar. If you're a nerd: it's all just Javascript on npm, you get to make your own stuff! If you're not a nerd: don't worry, you found this thing so you're nerd enough. Learn more

Increase your digital worth

The VR blockchain is mining coins for you -- right now. Find coins on servers and add them to your webwallet. Your coins are stored locally, so only you get to hold them, and use them to buy awesome things in VR. Learn more

Hack your metaverse

Make virtual stuff, publish to NPM, mine coins, mint assets, accept payments. The APIs are waiting for you to work your hacker magic. Either way, what's yours is yours. Your content and coins live in a P2P network. Learn more
