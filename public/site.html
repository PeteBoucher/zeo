Shared world building

Build beautiful VR worlds with your hands.
Share your creations in realtime with other avatars by giving them the URL.
All you need is a browser and a keyboard. Of course, it's better with a WebVR headset 😀.

Also great for developers

The whole thing is pure Javascript web server.
Every piece of your world is a module on npm running on a THREE.js entity-component system.
If that sounds complicated don't worry -- it's all drag-and-drop and DevTools.
The API is DOM + THREE.js on the front and node on the back, so it works with pretty much everything.

The sky is not the limit

It's all open source, so everything can be customized. Don't like the sky? Delete it.
You don't even need to be a developer: there's a growing ecosystem of hand-crafted modules to play with.
And there's an integrated world browser if you're feeling lonesome -- or adventurous.
